Q: What is this?

A: A game to practice the orders of operations with the help of some rappers

Q: How do I play?

A: Every problem prompt will have a solution ending with one of the four numbers below, players pick a number with the arrow keys or by clicking on a rapper face.

Q: Why these specific numbers?

A: Each number is near and dear to the respective rapper's heart

Q: Why 2048?

A: 2 + 3 + 305 + 1738 = 2048
